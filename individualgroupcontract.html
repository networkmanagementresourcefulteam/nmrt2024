 Contract Paper Type:

  What are the contract paper types?
♦ An agreement form also known as a contract document can be drawn up in a simple contract format or agreement format. This agreement/contracts can be classified for individuals or groups.
 Where can we find the contract paper type?
♦ On ITZ screen from NDB we can locate the different contract paper we have, considering on what the provider has signed upon agreement.
 How can we identify which ones are for group, individual, hospital base or FAC/ANC?
 "On ITZ screen from NDB we can find the following:"
♦ Practitioner Agreement Template (PAT), Independent Practitioner Agreement (IPA) or Simplified Physician Agreement (SPA) are made for individual providers
♦ Medical Group Agreement (MGA) or Small Medical Group Agreement (SMG) are made for a group

   Note: MGA Copy of contracts or Fee Schedules will need to be requested to the NAM by NetworkHelp

♦ Old UHN Individual Agreement (OUI) and Old UHC Group agreement (OUG) needs to be escalated to NAM (DO NOT PROVIDE THE NAM INFROMATION FOR PHYSICIAN/GROUP)
♦ Not participating on UHC agreement (NPA), meaning no agreement have been signed with United Health Group ♦ Wrap Agreement (WRP) when multiple provider are contracted with each different individual agreements, however are attached to the same TIN
♦ Out Patient Facility Agreement (OUT) refer to NAM for contractual assistance or demographic updates, unless it is a status inquiry, in which case will be worked by NetworkHelp
♦ Federal Quality Healthcare/Rural Health Clinic (FQHC/RHC) entity is considered a Facility, however they are treated as a group, such as demo update and locating the NAM should be the same process as a medical group, because these entities are   contracted on a medical group level.

Which attachment can I use for the above information?

♦ UHN_PTI_Business_Rules_J4099
♦ Contract_Paper_Type_by_Contract_Organization_J1014

 PTI Indicator:

 What are the PTI Indicator?

♦ Pay to Indicator, Directive in Network Database (NDB) to indicate which record claims should be used to pay
   Note: Keep in mind we do not discuss payment information, this is only to have a clear view of what we are reading of NDB

 Where can we find the PTI indicator?

♦ This can be located on ITZ screen from our Network Database (NDB)
 How can we identify which ones will be for group and which will be for individual?

♦ "When payment goes to individual provider, NDB should populate with the following:"
♦Y - This will indicate is a individual contract not linked to a WRP contract paper

♦ "When payment goes to the PTI Name (it can be a group or individual provider), NDB should populate with the following:"
♦G - This will indicate one of the following group contract paper (SMGA, MGA, OUG, SPG, FQH and WRP) for Medicare (M&R) and Medicaid (C&S)

   Note: E will indicates a new group getting setup in NDB, which eventually will update to G when they are fully contracted

♦O - This will indicate Non-Par, meaning none of the individual providers or group can be contract

♦ "When Payment goes to group, NDB should populate with the following:"
♦M - This will indicate there are multiple groups under TIN

 Moved

♦N
♦ - This will indicate that individual provider does not need to be credentialed or have contracts loaded in NDB, however they must be linked to the TIN with the following contract papers (SMGA, MGA, OUG, SPG, FQH and WRP)

   Note: If HCP request is requesting the providers participation effective date, we should be able to find this information on ITZ screen by clicking your F12 on the keyboard you should be looking for PROV_TIN_PAY_AFFIL Date

   If the individual servicing provider is found and is flagged for sanctions, the system will use the individual provider record for the claim instead of the group record
